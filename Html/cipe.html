Token Features:


Every transaction will be charged a 6% “tax” whenever someone sells their BIRD to prevent the collapse of token price when whales decide to sell. This 6% tax is divided into portions:



  • 2% will be used to add liquidity for BIRD
  • 2% will be distributed to staked $BIRD on Launchzone’s PoolX
  • 1% will be burned
  • 1% will be given to the development and marketing wallet.



Deflationary Mechanics



At present, BIRD is also burned in the following ways:



  • 0.05% of every trade made on $BIRD AMM.
  • 20% of BIRD spent on lottery tickets
  • 3% of every Prediction markets round is used to buy BIRD for burning
  • 2% of every yield harvest in the Auto BIRD Pool
  • 1% from tax
