Win


1. Win lottery and earn $BIRD

  • There are 2 lottery sessions per day, each lasting for 12 hours.
  • 1 $BIRD can be traded for 1 lottery ticket.
  • There is no limit on the total number of lottery tickets one user can purchase. But a user can only purchase a maximum of 50 lottery tickets at a time.
  • One lottery ticket has 4 digits.

    1. The lottery tickets that match all 4 digits of the lottery jackpot in the exact order get 50% of the pot.
    2. The lottery tickets that match all 3 digits of the lottery jackpot in the exact order get 20% of the pot.
    3. The lottery tickets that match all 2 digits of the lottery jackpot in the exact order get 10% of the pot.
    4. The remaining 20% is burned.


2. Earn $Matic with prediction market.


Guess the price of MATICUSDT for the day to earn rewards. An exact guess of the Matic Price could earn you $BIRD just for trying out as your own TA!


  • Users can choose to commit any amount of $BIRD they want to join the prediction market
  • Each prediction round lasts 5 minutes. When each round starts, the Matic Price at that moment is “locked”. Users predict whether the price at the end of the round is higher or lower than the “locked price”. If their prediction is right, they earn MATIC cryptocurrency. If their prediction is wrong, they lose the committed MATIC.
  • 3% of every Matic Price prediction round is used to buy back $BIRD for burning.
