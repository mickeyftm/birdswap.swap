How to Add Polygon MATIC Mainnet to MetaMask







Step 1

Open your MetaMask app and click on the Network selection button in the top right corner. Choose Custom RPC.



Step 2

Fill in the boxes as follows:

Network Name: Matic Mainnet


New RPC URL: https://rpc-mainnet.matic.network

Chain ID: 137


Symbol: MATIC


Block Explorer URL: https://explorer.matic.network/


Step 3


Click Save and you're done
